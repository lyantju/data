The folder contains two '.m' files and five '.xls' files. 
The five '.xls' files are rainfall and tidal level samples with duration of 1 hour, 3 hours, 6 hours, 12 hours and 24 hours respectively.
The two '.m' files are used to calculate the marginal distribution of a single variable and the joint distribution and design value of two variables respectively.
two '.m' files can be modified. By modifying the name of the '.xls' file at the path, the results of different durations can be calculated.
